ABOUT

Entrepreneurship Cell at NIT Hamirpur believes
that entrepreneurship is not just about starting
companies, but a pathway towards India’s socio-
economic development. Our vision is to make students
and faculty ‘entrepreneurial’ in every work that they
do. We hope to enable them to solve global challenges
as we see students asthe greatest capital and intend to
nurture them, provide them with opportunities for excellence.

Innovation and enterprising are the wheels that
steer the world into a brighter future. Great ideas
result into great undertakings which pave the way
for glaring success. In order to facilitate
this vision of promoting entrepreneurship in India,
E-Cell VNIT organizes StartUp Conclave,
a national-level business plan competition.

Frequently Asked Question

Why Should I Participate In StartUp Hunt?

Right from acknowledging potential of your idea, enhancing your business acumen, steering your idea in correct direction, scrutinizing your views which will pave your way for glaring success , this platform has it all!


When And Where Is The Final Round Of startUp Hunt?

Consortium is a 3 day Entrepreneurship Summit which will be held on 8th to 10th March ’19. The final round of Startup Conclave shall be on 9th and 10th March. Shortlisted teams will be invited to VNIT for the final pitching.


What Is The Eligibility For Participating in StartUp Hunt?

Any NIT or non-NITian team having a budding startup idea which you think has a prospective of emerging as a successful startup provided we give a momentum is welcome. NITians should participate in NIT Tier and all others should participate in the Open Tier. You can participate in a team with 2 to 5 members.


Is There a Separate Event For NITians and NON-NITians?

It is the same event for everyone though we have done segregation only to recognize the best ideas from NITs. The qualification scenarios will be in respective groups only and will continue till last round.


What Is The Last Date Of Registration?

Registrations will be open till 5th January 2019.


How Much Is The Registration Fee?

Registration fees for the participants is Rs. 300 per team.


About B-Plan in Second Round

A B-plan or business plan is a formal written document containing business goals, various methods about how can these be attained , time frame within which these goals will be achieved and your future targets. Generally we expect a detailed document consisting of around 30-40 pages which briefly describes the future course of your startup.


Who Will Conduct The Mentoring Session

We have veterans from entrepreneurial field to conduct mentoring sessions who will guide you and also answer all your queries related to your startup.
